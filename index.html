    CTS No. 4667, Old Mumbai-Pune Highway, Kalbhor Nagar, Akurdi, Pimpri-Chinchwad, Pune 411035. Akurdi Metro at 2 min, PVR & Unique Children's Hospital at 3 min, Elpro City Square Mall at 5 min, PCMC Station at 6 min. Direct access to Hinjawadi IT Park and Chakan industrial zone.

    2 BHK from 800 sqft (₹1.10 Cr*), 2.5 BHK 916 sqft (₹1.30 Cr*), 3 BHK 1265 sqft (₹1.79 Cr*), 4 BHK Suite 1840 sqft (₹2.64 Cr*), and exclusive 4 BHK + Private Terrace units at K-3 first floor with up to 917 sqft of private terrace. All prices are new-launch indicative rates.

    Designer Clubhouse with Arrival Plaza (Ballroom, Lounge, Cigar & Poker Room), Horizon Pool & Little Lagoon, double-height Gym & GroupX Studio, Box Cricket Arena, The Coliseum, Skateboard Park, Gala Garden, Children's Adventure Zone, Fireside Lounge, Musical Gathering Space, Champions Court, Theme Gardens, Bus Service Deck — 20+ amenities total on the landscape podium.
